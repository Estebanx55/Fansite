The Game

Terraria is a 2D sandbox styled game based in exploration,building,crafting,fighting and mining that can be played in solo or co-op. Is a free-sandbox but you will need to play a little of every mecanic if you want to evolve in the game and then you are free to go deep in what mecanic you like the most(explore,build,fight and more). The world of Terraria is full of different biomes with different mobs, have more than 500 weapons plenty of armors and bosses to evalue your strengh.
